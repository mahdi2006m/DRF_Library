Privacy Policy and User Data

update Last Updated: February 15, 2026

At City Digital Library, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Our goal is to provide a secure environment for searching academic resources and borrowing books. Please review this policy carefully before using our services.

1 Introduction

By using the services of City Digital Library website, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information in accordance with this policy. Personal information includes any data that can directly or indirectly identify you.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our digital library services. It also outlines your rights regarding your personal data and how you can contact us if you have any concerns.

2 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide better services:

  • Identity Information: Full name, national ID number, birth certificate number, and contact phone number (during membership registration).
  • Library Activity Data: Membership card number, loan history, due dates, and potential late fees.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages visited (to improve site performance and user experience).
  • Communication Data: Email address and phone number for sending return reminders or cultural event notifications.

We only collect information that is necessary for providing our services. You are not required to provide optional information, but it may enhance your user experience.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Managing the library loan system and tracking the status of borrowed books.
  • Contacting users regarding due dates, overdue items, and reservation confirmations.
  • Improving website performance and user interface based on user behavior and feedback.
  • Enforcing library rules and preventing misuse of resources and system abuse.
  • Analyzing usage patterns to develop new features and improve existing services.

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only when required by law or to protect our rights and property.

4 Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your personal data. These measures include encryption, secure servers, and restricted access to personal information.

However, please note that no method of transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot ensure its absolute security.

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

5 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies to store your browser settings and remember your login session. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide you with a better experience when using our services.

You may change your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may restrict some functionality of the site. Some features of our website may not function properly without cookies enabled.

We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device until they expire or you delete them) for various purposes including authentication and preference storage.

6 Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you at any time.
  • Right to Correct: You can update your personal information (like phone number or email) via your user dashboard.
  • Right to Delete: You can request account cancellation and deletion of your personal data, provided you have no outstanding books or fines.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit how we use your personal information in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing of your personal information in certain circumstances, including direct marketing.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be highlighted on this page with a "Last Updated" date and notification to registered users. We recommend reviewing this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Your continued use of our services after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
