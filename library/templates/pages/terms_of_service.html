3 Borrowing and Usage Rules

We have established the following rules for borrowing and using our library resources:

Physical Books

  • Standard loan period is 14 days, renewable up to 3 times if no other user has reserved the item.
  • Members may borrow up to 5 physical items at a time.
  • Users must ensure books are returned in the condition they were borrowed. Any damage may incur fees.

Digital Resources

  • Access to e-books, audiobooks, and databases is limited to personal use.
  • Sharing download credentials or files with non-members is strictly prohibited.
  • Digital items have an expiration date defined by the publisher and cannot be returned early via the app.

4 Fines and Penalties

Important: Outstanding fines or lost items may result in suspension of borrowing privileges.

  • Late Fees: A fee of $0.25 per day per item is charged for overdue physical books.
  • Lost Items: If an item is lost or damaged beyond repair, the user must pay the full replacement cost plus a $5 processing fee.
  • Non-payment: Accounts with unpaid fines exceeding $25 will be blocked and referred to a collection agency if unresolved after 90 days.
